My Most Memorable Trip

Hawaii

In my opinion, the vacation spot is Hawaii. Three years ago, I went to Hawaii with my husband and with my daughter. We spent about a week there. Somethihng I love the most about Hawaii is that the culture is different than any other state I have gone to in the US. I enjoy warm weather the most, and sitting by the pool everyday in Hawaii brought me so much peace. Everyone we met there were enjoying themselves.

In Hawaii I was able to wear dresses all week. I remember being really excited to shop for the most colorful dresses, especially since I cannot wear dresses everyday here in Saint Louis.

If I could choose one vacation spot for the rest of my life, I would definitely choose Hawaii. I hope to go there again soon with my family. Maybe even for a longer period of time! Last time we went to Big Island, so I would love to go to Mauii or Honolulu next.
